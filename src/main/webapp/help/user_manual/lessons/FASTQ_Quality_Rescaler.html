FASTQ Quality Rescaler

An overview of FASTX Analyses is available.

The FASTX Quality Rescaler updates the base quality scores in your sequence data to use the Phred33 scale adopted by the Sanger Centre and the NCBI Sequence Read Archive. Conversion from Illumina 1.3+ and Solexa is supported.

Zoom

Click Browse to select the previously uploaded file you want to convert. Click Convert Scoring to continue.

Zoom

Specify the scoring type used in your read library from the drop-down menu. Click Launch Job.

Enter a name and desciription for the job and click OK. See Perform Analyses for information about monitoring the process and where to find your results.
